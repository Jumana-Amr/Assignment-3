Petya Ransomeware

Protection:

The best strategy to avoid ransomware is to train and educate users. People should know what to do, what not to do, how to avoid ransomware, and how to report it. The most important part of any ransomware security strategy is regular data backups. They should Keep operating systems, security software and patches up to date for all devices. Advanced email security solutions protect against malicious attachments, documents, and URLs in emails that lead to ransomware. During the attack, you should turn the computer off and disconnect from the network Petya waits about an hour after infecting a system before rebooting and displaying a message that the file system is being “repaired.” If the machine is turned off immediately, some files may be saved.
