Proactive Protection:

The best strategies to avoid ransomware are:

  • Securirty Awareness and Training Program

    • Users must be trained and educated. People should know what to do, what not to do, how to avoid ransomware, and how to report it. This topic should be addressed in general security awareness training program.

  • Data Backup

    • The most important part of any ransomware security strategy is to have regular data backups.

  • Vulnerability and Patch Management

    • System admins as well as users should keep their operating systems, security software, and applications up to date. Patch and vulnerability management process should be developed and enforced. Regular scans should be scheduled to detect and identify computers and/or systems that are missing critical patches to be fixed.

  • Email Security

    • Advanced email security solutions can protect against malicious attachments, documents, and URLs in emails that lead to ransomware.
